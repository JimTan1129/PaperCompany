Recycle Paper For Money!

In our company Paper Saver we like to help save the world. We save the world by recycling used newspapers to create new inventions. Each ton of recycled paper can save 17 trees, 380 gallons of oil, three cubic yards of landfill space, 4,000 kilowatts of energy and 7,000 gallons of water! Americans use more than 67 million tons of paper per year, or about 580 pounds per person.
